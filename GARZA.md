# For Garza — What This Is and How It Works

*One page. No tech talk.*

## The idea

Thirty years of Houston dirt taught you things no map or county record
knows — which blocks flood no matter what FEMA says, which owners never
sell, what a lot's really worth before the appraisal district catches up.

Right now, all of that lives in one place: **your head**.

Jarvo is the team's voice assistant. It already knows the county's data
cold — every parcel, every owner, every flood zone. What it's missing is
your judgment. This is how we add it.

## How it works — you just talk

- **You work like normal.** Look at deals, talk through them out loud.
  When you drop a rule — *"pass, that street drains backwards"* — Jarvo
  asks: **"Want me to keep that as one of your rules?"** Yes or no. Done.
- **Sometimes it interviews you.** With a deal on the screen, it'll ask
  things like *"what would you check here that I didn't?"* You answer the
  way you'd teach a young guy riding shotgun.

## The rule card

Everything you teach it becomes one clean card:

> **The rule:** Don't trust lot lines east of Lockwood without a fresh survey.
> **Where it applies:** Denver Harbor / east side
> **You said it:** July 2026

Three promises about every card:

1. **Nothing gets written down until it reads the card back and you say
   "that's right."** No card without your OK.
2. **Your name stays on it.** When Jarvo uses your rule, it says out loud:
   *"Garza's rule on this block is…"* — every single time. It never
   passes your judgment off as its own, and it never mixes your calls in
   with the county's numbers.
3. **You can change your mind.** Any card can be fixed or torn up, any
   time. If you said it in 2026 and the market changed, we update it —
   the card always shows its date.

If one of your rules and the county's data ever disagree, Jarvo says so
out loud. That's not a problem — **that's the moment you're worth the
most**, because that's the call nobody else in Houston can make.

## The scoreboard

Here's the part we think you'll like. The book keeps score: every time one
of your rules shapes a decision on a real deal, it gets logged with how the
deal turned out. A year from now, nobody has to take anyone's word that
your instincts are good — **it's on paper, with your name on it.**

## What this is really about

The maps and the numbers, anyone can buy. What you know, nobody can.
This makes sure the crew still gets taught by you on days you're not in
the truck — and long after that too.

We're not replacing your judgment. We're making sure it outlives your
phone number.
